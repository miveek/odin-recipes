Burger

Description

This Tex-Mex burger with Cajun mayo recipe yields a full-flavor, beefy burger that will tantalize your taste buds. The Cajun mayonnaise is a jazzy way to spice up any basic bland burger.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 4 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, divided
  • 1 ⅓ pounds ground beef sirloin
  • ½ cup diced white onion
  • 1 jalapeño pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 slices pepper Jack cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 slices tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning in a small bowl; set aside.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Combine ground sirloin, onion, remaining 3 teaspoons Cajun seasoning, jalapeño, garlic, and Worcestershire sauce in a large bowl. Divide into 4 balls; flatten into patties.
  5. Cook on the preheated grill until burgers are no longer pink in centers, about 5 minutes per side, topping each burger with 1 cheese slice, during the last 2 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into centers should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Spread mayo on buns, add burgers, lettuce, and tomatoes.
